Instructions

1. Sign Up with the required details mentioned on the site.
2. Analyse the crop details and select the appropriate category for your investment.
3. The total expenses requested for a category are displayed. Enter the amount which you wish to invest.
4. You now hold a stake equivalent to the percentage of amount invested to the requested expenses.
5. You cannot liquidate your investment until the crop cycle for the invested category of crops is completed.
5. At the end of a crop cycle, you have to liquidate your investments. You receive a percentage of the total profits equivalent to the percentage mentioned in Step 4.
